Cereal Mascots

And whether or not I think I could beat them in a game of Mario Kart

Buzz the Bee

Buzz the Bee might win. He's a very perserverant and determined character. In Honey Nut Cheerios ads there would always be a villain that would try to steal the cereal from everyone, but Buzz would always be there to save the day. I think he would channel that determination into a game of Mario Kart so that even if he doesn't win the first round, he'd quickly become really good at it. I think I might end up losing the game against Buzz the Bee.

Tony the Tiger

Okay so originally I'd written that Tony would win for sure, because that was the initial vibe I got. But honestly, I think he would just act like he knows how to play, and then once the round begins he would immediately start driving in the wrong direction. I think I would win against Tony the Tiger.

The Trix Rabbit

I kind of feel bad for the Trix Rabbit. I don't think he's ever even had a taste of his own cereal.The kids in those ads were always unnecessarily cruel to this guy, like, what do you mean "Silly rabbit, Trix are for kids", its his cereal. He is on the box, let him have one bite, guys. I think he's probably very bad at Mario Kart, but I'd change up the settings and drive slower to make the round easier for him so he can at least have one win in his life.

Snap, Crackle, and Pop

These guys are probably really annoying. They'd probably whine and tell on me if I won a round. I'd avoid playing against these guys at all, but hypothetically if I were to play against them I think I'd win.

Toucan Sam

This would be a pretty fair game. Like, if I were to rank myself amongst the skill-level of these mascots I think I'm Toucan-Sam-level. I'll call this one a tie.

Cap'n Crunch

I don't think Cap'n would even be able to grasp the concept of a videogame. He'd probably be really confused about all the little characters that seem to be stuck inside the tv screen. I don't think he'd win in a game of Mario Kart.

Chip the Wolf

Chip is not winning.

The Dig'em Frog

I don't know if I can beat the Dig'em Frog. Look at his little sideways hat and tell me that isn't the outfit of a Mario Kart champion. He probably knows all the shortcuts and stuff too. I think the Dig'em Frog would win for sure.

The Raisin Bran Sun

This guy is evil. I can't explain it, but there's like a searing malice behind that gaze. Not to mention the fact that he's literally the sun and would probably incinerate me. He also has those scoops of raisins which I feel he could easily throw at me to mess me up.
